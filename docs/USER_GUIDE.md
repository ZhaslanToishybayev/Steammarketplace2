### What is Steam Marketplace?

## 📝 Account Setup

### Step 1: Register with Steam

1. Visit [Steam Marketplace](https://sgomarket.com)
2. Click **"Login with Steam"**
3. You'll be redirected to Steam's official login page
4. Enter your Steam credentials
5. Review and accept the permissions request
6. You'll be redirected back to our platform

### Step 2: Verify Your Email

After login, you'll receive a verification email:
1. Check your email (check spam folder if needed)
2. Click the verification link
3. You'll be logged in to your account

### Step 3: Secure Your Account

**Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. Click **"Enable 2FA"**
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app
4. Enter the verification code
5. Save your backup codes in a safe place

**Enable Steam Guard:**
1. In Steam, go to **Steam Guard** settings
2. Add your phone number
3. Download the Steam Mobile app
6. Confirm on your phone when prompted

### Step 4: Complete Your Profile

1. **Profile Photo**: Upload a profile picture (optional)
2. **Display Name**: Choose how others see you
3. **Bio**: Add a short bio (optional)
4. **Notification Preferences**: Choose what alerts to receive

## 💰 Wallet & Payments

### Adding Funds

1. Go to **Wallet** page
2. Click **"Add Funds"**
3. Choose payment method:
   - Credit/Debit Card (Visa, MasterCard, Amex)
   - PayPal
   - Bank Transfer (for large amounts)
4. Enter amount (minimum $10)
5. Complete payment with our secure payment processor (Stripe)
6. Funds appear in your wallet instantly

### Wallet Balance

**Available Balance**: Money you can use for purchases
**Pending Balance**: Money being processed or on hold

### Making Purchases

1. **Find Item**: Browse or search for an item
2. **Click Buy**: Click the **"Buy Now"** button
3. **Review**: Check the item, price, and fees
4. **Confirm**: Click **"Confirm Purchase"**
5. **Payment**: Use your wallet balance or add funds
6. **Success**: You'll receive a confirmation email

### Withdrawing Funds

**Requirements:**
- Account verified with 2FA
- At least one completed transaction
- No pending trades or issues

**Process:**
1. Go to **Wallet** → **Withdraw**
2. Enter amount (minimum $20)
3. Choose withdrawal method:
   - PayPal (1-3 business days)
   - Bank transfer (3-5 business days)
4. Confirm withdrawal
5. Check your email for confirmation

### Transaction History

View all your transactions:
1. Go to **Account** → **Transaction History**
2. Filter by:
   - Date range
   - Type (Purchase, Sale, Withdrawal)
   - Status (Completed, Pending, Failed)
3. Export to CSV or PDF

### Payment Security

- **SSL Encryption**: All payments are encrypted
- **PCI Compliance**: We never store your card details
- **Fraud Protection**: Advanced fraud detection
- **Dispute Resolution**: 24/7 support for payment issues

## 🔄 Trading

### How It Works

**When you buy:**
1. You purchase an item listing
2. We send a trade offer to the seller
3. Seller accepts the trade offer
4. You receive the item in your Steam inventory
5. Money is transferred to the seller

**When you sell:**
1. You create a listing with your item
2. Wait for a buyer
3. Accept the trade offer from the buyer
4. Send the item to the buyer
5. Receive payment in your wallet

### Creating a Listing

1. Go to **My Account** → **My Inventory**
2. Select items you want to sell
3. Click **"Create Listing"**
4. Set your price (or use suggested price)
5. Choose listing duration (1, 3, or 7 days)
6. Review and publish

**Price Tips:**
- Check the suggested market price
- Look at recent sales for similar items
- Consider item wear level (Factory New = higher price)
- Factor in the 5% marketplace fee

### Managing Your Listings

**View Active Listings:**
- Go to **My Account** → **My Listings**
- See status: Active, Pending, Sold, Expired
- Edit price or cancel if not sold yet

**Modify Price:**
1. Click on your active listing
2. Click **"Edit Price"**
3. Enter new price
4. Save changes

**Cancel Listing:**
1. Click on your active listing
2. Click **"Cancel Listing"**
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Item returns to your inventory

### Trade Offers

**When you receive a trade offer:**
1. Check your Steam mobile app or desktop
2. Review the offer carefully
3. Accept or decline

**Important:**
- **Only accept from our verified bots** (sgomarket_bot)
- Check the item matches what you ordered
- Never share your trade offer with others
- We never ask for your login credentials

### Trade Timeline

| Step | Action | Time |
|------|--------|------|
| 1 | Buyer purchases item | Instant |
| 2 | Trade offer sent to seller | 1-2 minutes |
| 3 | Seller accepts offer | Usually < 1 hour |
| 4 | Item sent to buyer | 1-5 minutes |
| 5 | Money released to seller | After trade completes |

**Total Time**: Usually under 1 hour, but can take up to 24 hours

## 🔒 Security

### Account Security Best Practices

1. **Enable 2FA**: Use an authenticator app
2. **Strong Password**: At least 12 characters, mix of letters/numbers/symbols
3. **Unique Password**: Don't reuse passwords from other sites
4. **Steam Guard**: Enable on your Steam account
5. **Email Security**: Use a strong email password
6. **Public WiFi**: Avoid logging in on public networks

### What We Do to Protect You

- **Encrypted Data**: All personal data is encrypted
- **Secure Payments**: PCI-compliant payment processing
- **Identity Verification**: Verify users with phone and email
- **Fraud Detection**: Monitor for suspicious activity
- **Dispute Resolution**: Help resolve issues quickly
- **Account Monitoring**: 24/7 monitoring for security threats

### Recognizing Scams

**Common Scams:**
1. **Fake Trade Offers**: Only accept from our verified bots
2. **Phishing Emails**: We'll never ask for your password
3. **Impersonation**: Always check the website URL
4. **Too Good to Be True**: Unrealistic prices are often scams
5. **Pressure Tactics**: Scammers rush you to make decisions

**Report Suspicious Activity:**
- Email: security@sgomarket.com
- Include: Screenshots, transaction IDs, details

### Steam Guard Requirements

**Why We Require Steam Guard:**
- Protects against account theft
- Prevents unauthorized trades
- Required by Steam's terms of service
- Keeps your items safe

**To Enable Steam Guard:**
1. Open Steam → Settings → Steam Guard
2. Add your phone number
3. Download Steam Mobile app
4. Confirm on your phone when prompted

## ❓ FAQ

### Account & Security

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Go to the login page, click "Forgot Password", enter your email, and follow the reset link.

**Q: I forgot my 2FA codes. What do I do?**
A: Use your backup codes. If you don't have those, contact support for identity verification.

**Q: Can I change my Steam account?**
A: No, Steam accounts are permanent. You can create a new marketplace account with a different Steam account.

**Q: Is my personal information safe?**
A: Yes, we use bank-level encryption and never share your information with third parties.

### Trading

**Q: How long do trades take?**
A: Most trades complete within 1 hour, but can take up to 24 hours depending on the seller's response time.

**Q: Can I cancel a trade after I buy?**
A: No, once you purchase, the transaction is binding. Only the seller can cancel by declining the trade offer.

**Q: What if the seller doesn't respond?**
A: We'll cancel the trade and refund you after 24 hours of no response from the seller.

**Q: Can I buy and sell the same item?**
A: Yes, you can create your own listings even for items you just bought.

### Payments

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: Credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for large amounts.

**Q: When do funds appear in my wallet?**
A: Instantly for credit/debit cards. 1-3 business days for PayPal and bank transfers.

**Q: Why is my withdrawal pending?**
A: Withdrawals can be pending due to: identity verification, bank processing, or security review.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Refunds are available for undelivered items or items not as described. Contact support within 7 days.

### Technical

**Q: The site isn't loading. What should I try?**
A: Clear your browser cache, try a different browser, check your internet connection. If still not working, contact support.

**Q: I see a 404 error. What does it mean?**
A: The page you're looking for doesn't exist. Try going back to the homepage or use the search function.

**Q: Can I use the marketplace on mobile?**
A: Yes! Our site is fully responsive. We also have iOS and Android apps in development.

**Q: Do you support multiple languages?**
A: Currently English only. More languages coming soon!

### Legal

**Q: Is this an official Steam partner?**
A: We're an independent platform that integrates with Steam's OpenID and trade APIs. We're not officially affiliated with Valve/Steam.

**Q: What countries do you serve?**
A: We serve most countries worldwide. Some regions have restrictions. Contact support to confirm.

**Q: Am I responsible for taxes?**
A: Yes, you're responsible for reporting income from sales. Consult a tax professional for your situation.

## 🎓 Glossary

**AK-47 / AK**: Avtomat Kalashnikova - Popular rifle
**AWP**: Arctic Precision Widowmaker - Sniper rifle
**Float Value**: Number from 0 to 1 indicating wear (0 = perfect, 1 = heavily worn)
**StatTrak**: Special version of a skin with a kill counter
**Souvenir**: Special version of a skin obtained from tournaments
**Case**: Container that contains random skins
**Market**: Steam Community Market (also refers to our marketplace)
**Bot**: Automated system for sending/receiving trades
**Rep**: Reputation - rating of a user based on completed trades
**Low Float**: Skin with very low wear, highly valued
**Pattern**: Visual design on the skin
**Pattern Index**: Number that determines the specific pattern
**Wear Level**: How worn the skin appears (Factory New, Minimal Wear, etc.)
